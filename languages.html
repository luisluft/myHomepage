I have always considered learning languages as very important part of my life. I believe it helps with memory retention and logical reasoning. Because of this I am always studying a new language.

Spanish

German

The language I am currently studying. It is a very hard and challenging language but it is fun and I enjoy the harsh way it sounds. I am studying it for 3 years at Berlin Idiomas and but think there will be a couple of more before I considered myself fluent in it.
